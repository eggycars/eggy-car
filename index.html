What is Eggy Car?

Eggy Car is a simple yet addictive game where the player must drive a car while keeping an egg balanced on its roof. The objective is to travel as far as possible without letting the egg fall. The game’s straightforward premise belies its challenging nature, requiring players to carefully navigate hills and obstacles while maintaining the egg’s stability.

How to Play Eggy Car

Playing Eggy Car is easy to grasp but hard to master. The controls are intuitive: you accelerate and decelerate using the right and left arrows, respectively. The challenge lies in the terrain and the egg's tendency to roll with the car’s movements. Mastering the balance between speed and caution is key to achieving high scores in this delightful game.
